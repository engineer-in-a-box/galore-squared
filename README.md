An addon for Space Age Galore that adds 45 more recipes

# Recipes:

### Casting superconductor
- ingredients:
    - 1 plastic bar
    - 1 copper plate
    - 15 holmium solution
    - 5 light oil
- results:
    - 2 superconductors
- crafting time: 2.5 sec
- category: electromagnetics
- allows productivity

### Iron cable
- ingredients:
    - 1 iron plate
- results:
    - 2 copper cables
- crafting time: 0.25 sec
- category: electronics
- allows productivity
